VM Check Ports

Help/About

What does this application do?
Allows you to obtain the status of communication ports on content servers on the network. I.e.: It informs you if your security camera is accessible for connections from outside your network.

How to use this application?
Fill the "Host" field with an IP address or a valid URL and the "Port" field with a port number between 1 and 65536 and click "check". If the service is Publicly available, the port will appear as open.

Who is this application for?
It is useful for IT professionals and electronic security technicians.
